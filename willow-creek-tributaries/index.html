BACKGROUND

The Willow Creek Tributaries Major Drainageway Plan and FHAD are contracted through the Mile High Flood District. There are many constituents and entities within the study area that stretches from the Englewood Dam to just south of C470 in Douglas County. Direct stakeholders include MHFD, SEMSWA, the City of Lone Tree, the City of Centennial, Douglas County, Arapahoe County, South Suburban Parks and Recreation District, and Southgate Sanitation District.

The primary goals for the Willow Creek Tributaries Major Drainageway Plan and FHAD are to update hydrology upstream of the Englewood Dam and update flood hazard delineations. This study intends to identify problems in the watershed regarding stream health and resiliency, flooding, water quality, maintenance needs, health and safety, equity, and other utilities and infrastructure.
